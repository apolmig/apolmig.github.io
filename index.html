Acerca de mí

I believe in Artificial Intelligence for All ⟺ Artificial Intelligence for Good. Currently serving as AI Advisor at Spain's Office of the Prime Minister (La Moncloa), where I spearhead initiatives on Community, Applications, and Infrastructure to accelerate AI transformation at the national level.

As Founder of Saturdays.AI since 2018, I've built the largest AI education community in Spain with 30k+ students across 10 countries, running applied machine learning bootcamps where participants build social impact, open-source AI projects. We've created initiatives like DAWIA (AI as instructors in higher education), FPIA (helping institutions become AI-First), LazaroAI (preserving memories for neurodegenerative diseases), RefugeesLive (AI tools for refugee integration), and LearniaClub (educate through entertainment).

My background spans 12 years at Repsol in various technical and management roles, from Operations Supervisor managing 14M tons per year of hydrocarbons to IT Analyst and Project Manager overseeing $300M+ projects. I've also taught Machine Learning and AIoT at EPITECH, led AI development as Manager at Stealth companies, and served as Interim CTO at TalentYou developing LLM-powered platforms. I'm multilingual (Spanish/Catalan native, English professional, French limited), hold an MBA from HEC Paris, completed postgraduate studies at Wharton School, and am an Acumen Fellow focused on social impact.
